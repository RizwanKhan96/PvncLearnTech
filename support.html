Quick Troubleshooting

Common issues and quick fixes:

Visit Microsoft's password reset page and follow the prompts. If you're unable to reset it yourself, contact the Help Desk at 705-748-1811 or email helpdesk@pvnccdsb.on.ca with your name and account details.

First, ensure it's charged ‚Äì plug it in for at least 30 minutes. Try holding the power button for 10-15 seconds. If still unresponsive, contact the Help Desk immediately, especially if you need the device for teaching or urgent work.

Try turning Wi-Fi off, then on again on your device. Make sure you're connecting to the correct network (PVNCCDSB-Staff or PVNCCDSB-Student). If issues persist, check with colleagues to see if it's a widespread problem, then contact the Help Desk.

Check that it's powered on and the correct input source is selected. Ensure cables are securely connected. Try restarting the projector and your computer. If unresolved, call the Help Desk (705-748-1811) immediately as this is a high-priority issue affecting instruction.

Submit a request through the Help Desk portal at pvnccdsb.ebasefm.com or email helpdesk@pvnccdsb.on.ca with software name and version. Allow 2-5 business days for evaluation.

Check your internet connection first. Try accessing email from a different device or browser. Check your spam/junk folder. If the issue persists, contact the Help Desk immediately, especially if you need the device for teaching or urgent work.

Report immediately to your school office or contact the Help Desk. Provide your name, device tag/serial number, and description of damage or loss. This is high-priority so appropriate action can be taken.

What Information to Include

Providing complete information helps us resolve your issue faster. When contacting the Help Desk, please include:

Required Information

  • Your name and contact information (email and phone number)
  • Your role (Teacher, student, staff, administrator)
  • Your school or location
  • Device information if applicable (asset tag, serial number, or device name)
  • Clear description of the problem ‚Äì what's not working?
  • When the problem started ‚Äì was it sudden or gradual?
  • What you've already tried ‚Äì any troubleshooting steps taken?

Helpful Additional Information

  • Error messages ‚Äì exact wording or screenshots
  • Screenshots or photos of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce ‚Äì what actions lead to the problem?
  • Frequency ‚Äì does it happen every time or intermittently?
  • Impact ‚Äì how is this affecting your work?
  • Urgency ‚Äì do you need this resolved by a specific date/time?
