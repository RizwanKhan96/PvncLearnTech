Common Issues & Quick Fixes

üî¥ Cannot Access Google Classroom

If you cannot access Google Classroom:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try accessing in an incognito/private window
  • Check if you're signed in with the correct account
  • If still unable, call Help Desk immediately

‚ö†Ô∏è Forgot Your Password

To reset your PVNCCDSB password:

  • Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  • Enter your email address
  • Follow the reset link sent to your email
  • Create a new strong password

üìÑ How to Set Up Your Device

For new device setup instructions:

  • Check Resources Library for setup guides
  • Visit our Learning Technologies website
  • Contact your school's IT specialist
  • Submit a support ticket for personalized help

üìÑ Getting Access to a New Tool

To request access to educational software:

  • Submit a ticket with the tool name and reason
  • Include your role (teacher, admin, student)
  • Wait for approval and access configuration
  • Allow 24-48 hours for processing

‚ö†Ô∏è Internet Connection Issues

Troubleshoot connectivity problems:

  • Restart your device and router
  • Check if other devices can connect
  • Forget and reconnect to WiFi network
  • Contact IT Help Desk if issue persists

üî¥ Printer Not Working

Quick printer troubleshooting:

  • Check printer is powered on and connected
  • Verify printer shows on your device's printer list
  • Remove and re-add printer if missing
  • Submit ticket or call Help Desk for hardware issues

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ñ∂ What should I do if I think my account has been compromised?

If you suspect your account has been compromised, immediately contact the IT Help Desk. Change your password from a secure device, and do not click any suspicious links or attachments. The Help Desk can review account activity and secure your account.

‚ñ∂ Can I install software on my school device?

Most school devices have restrictions to maintain security and prevent conflicts. If you need specific software, submit a request through the Help Desk with details about why you need it. Approved software will be installed by IT staff.

‚ñ∂ How do I back up my files?

All staff and student accounts have access to Google Drive which automatically backs up files. For additional local backups, contact the Help Desk to discuss backup solutions. Learn more in our Resources Library.

‚ñ∂ What's the Help Desk response time for urgent issues?

Urgent issues reported by phone during business hours receive immediate attention. Tickets submitted online typically receive responses within 24-48 hours. Critical system outages affecting multiple schools are prioritized immediately.

‚ñ∂ Can I use personal devices on the school network?

PVNCCDSB provides guest network access for personal devices. You cannot connect personal devices to the staff network without IT approval. The guest network allows internet access but not access to school resources. Contact your school's IT coordinator for details.

üìß Still Need Help?

If you haven't found an answer, please reach out to us through any of the methods above. You can also contact your school's assigned Learning Technologies Specialist for instructional technology support, or the Help Desk for hardware and software issues.
