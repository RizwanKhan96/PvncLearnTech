Quick Troubleshooting

Common issues and quick fixes:

Try these steps first:

  1. Clear your browser cookies and cache, then try again
  2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  3. Ensure CAPS LOCK is off and you're using your full email (firstname.lastname@pvnccdsb.on.ca)
  4. Check if you're connected to the internet
  5. If still unable to log in, contact IT Help Desk with your username

Try these steps first:

  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  4. Try in a different browser or in Incognito/Private mode
  5. Wait 5-10 minutes (may be a temporary service issue)
  6. Check Google's service status page

Steps to reset your PVNCCDSB password:

  1. Go to Google Account Recovery
  2. Enter your PVNCCDSB email address
  3. Verify your identity using a recovery email or phone number
  4. Create a new password (make it strong: mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)
  5. If you can't verify your identity, contact IT Help Desk

Try these solutions:

  1. Check you have sufficient storage space in Google Drive (check from drive.google.com)
  2. Check file size (try uploading a smaller file first)
  3. Check your internet connection speed
  4. Try uploading from a different folder/location
  5. Refresh the page and try again
  6. If the problem persists, contact IT Help Desk
