Quick Troubleshooting

Before Contacting Support, Try These Steps

1. Internet Connection Issues

  • Restart your device (wait 30 seconds before turning back on)
  • Check if other devices can connect to the network
  • Reconnect to WiFi or wired connection
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

2. Cannot Access Google Classroom

  • Verify you're logged in with your PVNCCDSB account (@pvnccdsb.on.ca)
  • Try accessing from a different browser
  • Clear browser cache and refresh the page
  • Check that classroom code is entered correctly

3. Password Reset

  • Visit password.pvnccdsb.on.ca
  • Click "I forgot my password"
  • Enter your PVNCCDSB email address
  • Follow instructions sent to your recovery email

4. Chromebook Issues

  • Sign out and sign back in
  • Restart your Chromebook (Power + R)
  • Check Getting Started with Chromebooks guide
  • If still not working, contact IT with specific error messages

Still having issues? Contact IT using one of the methods above and provide:

  • Description of the problem
  • Steps you've already tried
  • Any error messages (screenshots helpful)
  • Device type and browser name
