Technology support information

This page explains how to request technology support within PVNCCDSB. Support is coordinated through the board's official helpdesk ticket system.

Submit a support ticket

Submitting a ticket through the official helpdesk system is the fastest way to get help. Tickets are tracked and routed to the appropriate support team.

Please include as much detail as you can, such as the school, device type, and a clear description of the issue.

Open helpdesk ticket

Contacting the helpdesk by email

If you cannot access the ticket system directly, you can email the helpdesk. Include your name, school, contact information, and a clear description of the issue.

Email: helpdesk@example.org

What types of issues can I submit?

The helpdesk can assist with many different types of issues, including:

• Problems signing in to a device or Google account
• Issues with Chromebooks or classroom devices
• Network access and Wi‑Fi connection issues
• Questions about using board-approved tools in the classroom

For classroom practice questions or ideas about using technology in teaching, you can also explore the Technology resources library.

Support hours

Typical helpdesk hours are during regular school days. Exact hours may vary, but tickets can be submitted at any time.

Response times depend on issue type and volume. Urgent issues affecting many classrooms may be prioritized.

Emergency situations

For urgent, board-wide incidents or safety-related situations, please follow your school's established emergency communication procedures. The helpdesk ticket system should be used for technology issues that can be queued and processed.
