This comprehensive guide will help you get started with your PVNCCDSB Chromebook. Learn how to sign in, navigate the interface, and access essential applications.

What is a Chromebook?

A Chromebook is a laptop that runs on Chrome OS, an operating system designed by Google. Chromebooks are:

  • Fast - Boot up in seconds and run smoothly
  • Secure - Built-in virus protection and automatic updates
  • Simple - Easy to use with a familiar Chrome browser interface
  • Cloud-based - Your files are saved to Google Drive automatically
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note

Chromebooks are managed by PVNCCDSB IT. This means certain settings and installations are controlled to keep your device secure and working properly.

First-Time Setup

When you receive your Chromebook for the first time, follow these steps:

1

Charge Your Chromebook

Before using your Chromebook, plug it in and let it charge fully. The charging indicator light will show when it's charging and when the battery is full.

2

Power On

Press the power button (usually located in the top-right corner of the keyboard) to turn on your Chromebook.

3

Select Your Language and Network

Choose your preferred language and connect to a Wi-Fi network:

  • At school: Connect to PVNC-Student or PVNC-Staff
  • At home: Select your home Wi-Fi network and enter the password

Signing In to Your Chromebook

1

Enter Your PVNCCDSB Email

On the sign-in screen, enter your PVNCCDSB Google Workspace account:

  • Students: studentnumber@mypvnccdsb.ca (e.g., 123456@mypvnccdsb.ca)
  • Staff: firstname.lastname@pvnccdsb.on.ca
2

Enter Your Password

Type your PVNCCDSB account password and click Next.

Forgot your password? See our Password Reset Guide or contact your teacher/IT support.

‚úÖ You're Signed In!

Once signed in, your Chromebook will load your personalized desktop with access to all your Google Workspace apps and settings.

Basic Navigation

The Desktop

Your Chromebook desktop includes:

  • Wallpaper - The background image (customizable)
  • Shelf - The taskbar at the bottom showing pinned apps
  • Launcher - The circle icon to access all your apps
  • Status Area - Time, battery, Wi-Fi, and profile icon in the bottom-right corner

Essential Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Search key + L - Lock your Chromebook
  • Ctrl + T - Open a new browser tab
  • Ctrl + W - Close current tab
  • Ctrl + Shift + Q (twice) - Sign out
  • Search key + Arrow - Snap window to side
  • Alt + Tab - Switch between open windows

Touchpad Gestures

  • One finger tap - Click
  • Two finger tap - Right-click
  • Two finger scroll - Scroll up/down pages
  • Three finger swipe up - See all open windows

Essential Apps

1

Google Chrome

Your main web browser for accessing websites, online resources, and web-based applications. Chrome is always available and automatically opens when you click most links.

2

Google Classroom

Access your classes, view assignments, submit work, and communicate with teachers. Find it in your app launcher or go to classroom.google.com.

3

Google Drive

Store and access all your files in the cloud. Your work automatically saves to Drive, so you can access it from any device.

4

Google Docs, Sheets, & Slides

Create documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. These apps work like Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint but save automatically to your Drive.

5

Gmail

Access your PVNCCDSB email account to communicate with teachers and classmates. Available at gmail.com or through the Gmail app.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues with your Chromebook:

  • Won't turn on: Make sure it's charged - plug it in for at least 30 minutes
  • Can't connect to Wi-Fi: Make sure you're selecting the correct network (PVNC-Student or PVNC-Staff)
  • Forgot password: See our Password Reset Guide
  • Screen is frozen: Hold the power button for 10 seconds to force restart
  • Apps won't open: Try restarting your Chromebook
