This guide explains how teachers, principals, and vice-principals can use the PVNC Self-Service Password Reset Portal to reset student passwords quickly and efficiently.

Overview

The PVNC Self-Service Password Reset Portal is a secure web-based tool that allows authorized staff members to reset student passwords without submitting a help desk ticket. This streamlined process saves time and gets students back into their accounts faster.

Portal URL:

https://password.pvnccdsb.on.ca

Who Can Use This Portal

The password reset portal is available to:

  • Teachers - Can reset passwords for students in their classes
  • Principals - Can reset passwords for all students in their school
  • Vice-Principals - Can reset passwords for all students in their school
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note

You must register for portal access before you can reset student passwords. Registration is a one-time process that takes just a few minutes.

First-Time Registration

Before you can reset student passwords, you need to register your account with the portal. Follow these steps:

1

Access the Portal

Navigate to the password reset portal:

https://password.pvnccdsb.on.ca
2

Click "Register"

On the login page, click the "Register" button to begin the registration process.

3

Enter Your Information

Complete the registration form with the following information:

  • Email Address: Your PVNCCDSB email address (firstname.lastname@pvnccdsb.on.ca)
  • Username: Your network username (typically firstname.lastname)
  • Security Questions: Select and answer security questions that you'll use to verify your identity
4

Verify Your Email

Check your PVNCCDSB email inbox for a verification message. Click the verification link in the email to complete your registration.

‚úÖ Registration Complete!

Once your email is verified, you can log in to the portal and begin resetting student passwords.

Resetting Student Passwords

Once you've registered, resetting a student password is quick and easy:

1

Log In to the Portal

Go to https://password.pvnccdsb.on.ca and log in with your PVNCCDSB credentials.

2

Search for the Student

Use the search function to find the student by:

  • Student name
  • Student number
  • Username

Note: You can only search for and reset passwords for students you have permission to manage (students in your classes or school).

3

Generate New Password

Click the "Reset Password" button next to the student's name. The system will:

  • Generate a new temporary password
  • Display the password on screen
  • Apply the new password to the student's account immediately
4

Provide Password to Student

Write down or copy the temporary password and provide it to the student. Make sure to:

  • Provide the password securely (don't email or post it publicly)
  • Instruct the student to log in with the temporary password
  • Remind the student they may be prompted to change the password on first login

Ready to Get Started?

Access the password reset portal now to register or reset student passwords

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues with the password reset portal:

  • Cannot register: Verify you're using your correct PVNCCDSB email address and username
  • Cannot find a student: Ensure you have permission to manage that student (they must be in your class or school)
  • Password reset not working: The password change may take a few minutes to synchronize across all systems
  • Account locked: Contact the IT Help Desk for assistance
