Resources and guidance for using technology in PVNCCDSB classrooms. Find simple step-by-step guides, quick reference documents, and support information.

How this site can help

This site is focused on practical, easy-to-follow resources that support classroom use of technology across our Catholic schools.

All tools and guides are based on our Google ecosystem, including Chromebooks, Google Workspace for Education, and board-approved applications.
