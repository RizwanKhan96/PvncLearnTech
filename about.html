‚ÑπÔ∏è About Learning Technologies

The Learning Technologies department is committed to supporting effective technology integration in teaching and learning across all PVNCCDSB schools. Our team of specialists provides training, resources, and support to help staff and students use technology to enhance Catholic education.

Our Mission

The Learning Technologies team empowers educators and students to leverage technology effectively in support of learning, teaching, and Catholic values. We provide:

  • Professional Development: Training and workshops on educational technology tools and best practices
  • Curriculum Support: Assistance with integrating technology into curriculum planning and delivery
  • Technical Guidance: Help with troubleshooting and effective use of educational technology
  • Innovation & Research: Exploration of emerging technologies and innovative teaching practices
  • Resources & Documentation: Guides, tutorials, and support materials for staff and students

Each Learning Technologies Specialist is assigned to specific schools and works closely with administrators, teachers, and students to ensure technology enhances learning outcomes.

How to Work With Us

Individual Support

Contact your assigned Learning Technologies Specialist directly via email to book one-on-one consultations, classroom visits, or tech support for instructional technology.

School-Based Professional Development

Work with your administrator to schedule school-based PD sessions. Specialists can deliver workshops on specific tools, strategies, or curriculum-integrated technology use.

Board-Wide Training

Attend board-wide training sessions and workshops - check our Training Calendar for upcoming opportunities.

Online Resources

Access our Resources Library for self-paced guides, video tutorials, and documentation on various educational technology tools and strategies.
