About Learning Technologies

Learning Technologies is a team of professionals within IT Services, working with staff at PVNCCDSB to embed technology that supports digital literacy, creativity, innovation, collaboration, and learning needs of all students.

Our mission

We support teaching and learning by providing guidance, resources, and technical assistance for using technology effectively in Catholic education.

Our work focuses on Google Workspace for Education, Chromebooks, and board-approved digital tools that enhance classroom instruction and student engagement.
