About Learning Technologies

Supporting teaching and learning with technology across the Peterborough Victoria Northumberland and Clarington Catholic District School Board.

Our Mission

The Learning Technologies department exists to support effective teaching and learning through the thoughtful integration of technology in Catholic education.

We work alongside educators, administrators, and students to ensure that technology serves as a tool for growth, creativity, and connection—always grounded in our Catholic values and commitment to the whole person.

What We Do

Supporting Educators

We provide practical support, training, and resources to help teachers use technology confidently and effectively in their classrooms.

  • Professional learning sessions on Google Workspace for Education
  • One-on-one coaching and troubleshooting
  • Classroom technology setup and support
  • Curriculum-aligned technology integration strategies

Supporting Students

We ensure students have the tools, access, and skills they need to learn and grow in a technology-rich environment.

  • Chromebook distribution and device management
  • Student account setup and access support
  • Digital citizenship and online safety education
  • Technical troubleshooting and helpdesk support

Infrastructure and Systems

Behind the scenes, we maintain the systems and infrastructure that make teaching and learning possible.

  • Google Workspace administration and security
  • Network connectivity and Wi-Fi access
  • Device management and inventory
  • Educational software and application management

Our Values

Technology in Catholic education is never just about devices or software. Our work is guided by principles that honor the dignity of each person and the mission of Catholic schools.

Accessibility

Every student and staff member deserves equitable access to the tools and support they need to succeed.

Simplicity

Technology should serve learning, not complicate it. We focus on practical, user-friendly solutions.

Stewardship

We care for the resources entrusted to us—devices, data, and the time and attention of those we serve.

Integrity

We promote safe, responsible, and faith-filled use of technology that respects human dignity.

Our Google Ecosystem

PVNCCDSB uses Google Workspace for Education as the foundation of our technology environment. This includes:

  • Chromebooks – Primary student devices across all schools
  • Google Classroom – Assignment distribution, feedback, and communication
  • Google Drive – Cloud storage and file organization
  • Gmail – Email for staff and students (where appropriate)
  • Google Docs, Sheets, Slides – Collaborative creation and editing
  • Google Meet – Video conferencing and virtual learning

This integrated ecosystem allows for seamless collaboration, accessibility from any device, and simplified management for our schools.
